# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Industrial buyers, facilities teams, distributors, and operators who need to
replace hazardous legacy chemicals with safer VertKleen products. They arrive
with a job, a current chemical, a surface, and a volume; the interface must help
them find the right product, review proof, request documentation, and move into
quote or checkout without chemistry-first friction.

## Product Purpose

MASEST is a commerce and proof platform for VertKleen products. It should make
product matching, field evidence, cart review, quote routing, and checkout feel
like one clear buyer workflow. Success means fewer dead ends, fewer mismatched
products, and a faster handoff from interest to priced order or informed quote.

## Brand Personality

Calm, technical, practical. The tone should feel direct and field-tested:
confident enough for facilities work, plain enough for buyers comparing a
known product replacement.

## Anti-references

Avoid chemistry-first catalog sheets, decorative SaaS dashboards, generic
landing-page hero bloat, nested cards, vague proof claims, and checkout flows
that hide whether an item is buyable or quote-only.

## Design Principles

1. Start with the buyer's job, not the molecule.
2. Keep proof close to the product decision.
3. Make buy versus quote state unmistakable.
4. Prefer dense, scannable controls over decorative space.
5. Route the user forward from every commerce state.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Preserve keyboard access, readable focus states, plain labels,
stable layouts at mobile widths, and clear non-color-only status messaging.
