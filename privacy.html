Buyer data stays scoped to buyer work.

MASEST uses contact, quote, account, order, and site-activity data to respond to requests, support procurement, operate the storefront, and improve the VertKleen buyer path.

What MASEST collects

Quote and contact forms may collect name, company, email, phone, industry, application notes, product interest, location, volume, and timing. Account and checkout flows may collect company setup details, addresses, order records, payment-setup status, and buyer messages.

How data is used

MASEST uses this data to answer requests, route documents, prepare quotes, process orders, manage buyer accounts, prevent abuse, and maintain support records. Newsletter signups are used to send VertKleen field updates and program notes.

Analytics and chat

The site uses first-party pageview and funnel-event tracking without intentional personal-data collection. Live chat, when enabled, may route conversation context through the chat provider so MASEST can respond to buyer questions.

Retention and sharing

Records are retained as needed for customer service, procurement history, accounting, compliance, and operational security. MASEST does not sell personal data. Service providers may process data only to operate site, email, chat, payment, accounting, and CRM workflows.
