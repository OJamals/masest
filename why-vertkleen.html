Every plant fights the same enemies.

Scale that chokes heat transfer. Rust that eats steel. Grease that feeds fire risk. Bacteria that wait in warm water.

Left alone, the system chokes.

Minerals crystallize into scale. Steel oxidizes into rust. Grease films harden. Biofilm takes hold. Layer by layer the walls close in, and every layer costs energy, capacity, and safety.

The fix is chemistry, and chemistry gets scored.

Crews fight buildup with acids, caustics, and biocides. HMIS scores danger with three numbers from 0 to 4: health, flammability, and reactivity.

Now score the chemicals doing this work.
